Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

We need to innovate to solve the problems of the country. We need to come up with new ideas and new ways of doing things.

Idea

We need to have big ideas for the future of Bangladesh. We need to think outside the box and come up with creative solutions to our problems.

Vision

We need to have a clear vision for the future of Bangladesh. We need to know where we want to go and how we are going to get there.

Challenge

We need to be willing to take on the challenges that come our way. We need to be brave and not afraid to fail.
